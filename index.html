WELCOME TO OUR HOTEL

It is with great pleasure we introduce ourselves as "Hotel Three Star Pvt Ltd" a professionally managed residential hotel.

Our hotel opened on March 16, 2007 with 43 Deluxe Rooms at Hiranandani's Crystal Plaza, Kharghar on the ever important Mumbai Pune Highway in the Heart of Navi Mumbai. We are pleased to inform you that our hotel was inaugurated by the Honorable Governor of Maharashtra Shri S.M. Krishna and The Chief Minister of the State Shri Vilasrao Deshmukh.

Hotel Three Star Pvt. Ltd. has been approved by The Government of India 'Department of Tourism' and is the "First Three Star Classified Hotel" in Raigad District Navi Mumbai.It is located at Crystal Plaza, Plot No.18/27, Sector- 7, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai.

Looking forward to extend our warm hospitality to you'.
